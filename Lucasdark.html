Lucas

Origin

Lucas is a boys' name of Greek descent, derived from the name "Loucas". It means "bringer of light", and is an extremely popular name for Christian boys in the west. There is also an alternate derivation of the word, meaning "man from Lucania." Lucania was an ancient region of southern Italy thought to be the bringer of all light in the Roman Empire.
