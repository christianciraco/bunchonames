Vito

Origin

Vito is a boy's name of Latin and mediterranean origin meaning "life." It stems from both the Italian word "vita" (meaning "life") and the Latin name "Vitus" meaning "life-giver", and also the name of the patron saint of dogs. It is used as a very common name in regions of Italy.
