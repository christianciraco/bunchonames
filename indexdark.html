Lucas

Lucas is a name of Latin origin meaning "bringer of light" It is most often taken up by Roman Catholics as a name of choice.

Why Names Are Important

Names are one of the defining characteristics of our modern society. They hold so much significance and information, including our culture, our history, and our family history. All in all, names are the mostt important defining trait of a person besides their personality.
